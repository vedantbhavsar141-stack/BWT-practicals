About computer

A computer is a programmable electronic device that accepts data, processes it to perform calculations or other operations, and produces an output, often storing the results in the process. Considered one of the most significant inventions, computers have become indispensable to modern life, used across various fields like education, communication, business, healthcare, and entertainment. They operate on binary code (0s and 1s), but their user-friendly interfaces allow humans to input information in high-level languages, which the computer then converts to perform tasks like sending messages, managing data, and accessing vast amounts of information online.
